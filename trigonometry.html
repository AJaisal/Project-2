Right Triangle & SOH-CAH-TOA

The right triangle is where trigonometry truly begins. Use this mnemonic to remember trig ratios:

  • Sine = Opposite / Hypotenuse
  • Cosine = Adjacent / Hypotenuse
  • Tangent = Opposite / Adjacent

Visual labels make it easier to see how these ratios emerge from real triangles.
