Welcome to Precalculus

Precalculus bridges algebra, geometry, and calculus. This is where patterns deepen, graphs come alive, and functions start dancing across the coordinate plane.

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced functions and transformations
  • Exponential and logarithmic relationships
  • Trigonometric functions and graphs
  • Limits and concepts leading into calculus
  • Analytic geometry and conic sections

Think of precalculus as the final climb before the summit of calculus. Build strong skills here, and you'll breeze into the next level.

Helpful Reminders

  • Review algebra and trig basics often
  • Sketch graphs to see patterns clearly
  • Practice rewriting expressions different ways
  • Take your time with new functions
